3. Detailed Explanation of the Missing Capabilities

Here is the expanded explanation of each gap you identified.

A. Missing: Full Equity Vesting Engine

Right now you have:

Vesting start date

Vesting end date

But a real vesting engine requires:

Vesting schedules (monthly, quarterly, annual)

Cliff logic (e.g., 1‑year cliff)

Acceleration rules (single‑trigger, double‑trigger)

Grant lifecycle (granted → vested → exercised → expired)

Forfeiture rules

Termination handling

Automatic vesting calculations

Integration with payroll/tax for exercises

Without this, you cannot support:

ESOPs

RSUs

Options

Founder vesting

Employee equity plans

This is a major missing piece for a full equity platform.

B. Missing: Option/ESOP Exercise Workflow

You have transactions, but not:

Exercise requests

Strike price payments

Tax withholding

Exercise approvals

Certificate generation

Ledger posting for exercises

Updating cap table automatically

This is essential for any company with employees.

C. Missing: Dilution Waterfall & Scenario Modeling

You have rounds and share classes, but not:

Pre‑money vs post‑money modeling

Pro‑rata rights

Anti‑dilution (full ratchet, weighted average)

Liquidation preference modeling

Waterfall distribution engine

Scenario modeling (e.g., “What if we raise $5M at $20M pre?”)

This is the core value of advanced equity platforms like Carta or Pulley.

D. Missing: Multi‑Step Approvals & Internal Controls

Right now:

No approval matrix

No segregation of duties

No audit workflow

Enterprise finance requires:

Multi‑level approvals (preparer → reviewer → approver)

Role‑based approval rules

Delegation of authority

Audit trails for every approval step

Locking periods

Change‑management logs

Without this, you cannot serve:

Corporates

Auditors

Regulated entities
